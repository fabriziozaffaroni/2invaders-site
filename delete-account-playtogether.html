Account Deletion Request — PlayTogether

Last updated: July 2, 2026

If you wish to permanently delete your PlayTogether account and all data associated with it, you can submit a request by sending us an email at the address below.

Request Account Deletion

What gets deleted

Upon receiving your request we will permanently delete:

  • Your account credentials (email and hashed password)
  • Your username and profile avatar
  • Your friend list and pending challenge requests
  • Your match history
  • Your device push token

What to include in your email

  • Your in-app username
  • The email address used to register your account

This information is needed to locate and delete the correct account.

Processing time

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days of receiving your email.
