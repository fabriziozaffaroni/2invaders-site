Privacy Policy for PlayTogether

Last updated: July 2, 2026

PlayTogether ("the App") is a mobile multiplayer game developed by Fabrizio Zaffaroni ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

The App collects the following data:

  • Account information: email address and password (hashed), used to create and authenticate your account via Supabase.
  • Username: a display name you choose when setting up your profile.
  • Device push token: a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to send you game challenge notifications. This token is stored on our backend and is not shared with third parties beyond what is necessary to deliver the notification.
  • Gameplay data: match results (win/loss), room codes used to join games, and real-time game state during an active session. Real-time game state is ephemeral and is not persisted after the session ends.
  • Social data: your friend list (stored as pairs of user identifiers) and pending/accepted challenge requests.
  • Profile avatar: if you choose to set a profile picture, the App accesses your camera to take a selfie. The image is sent to our backend (a Supabase Edge Function) solely to generate a pixel-art avatar using Google Gemini AI. The original selfie is processed in transit and is not stored permanently by us. The resulting generated avatar image is stored and displayed to other players in your friend list and in the lobby.

The App does not collect precise location, contacts, microphone input, or payment information. Setting a profile avatar is optional.

2. How We Use the Data

  • Account information is used solely to authenticate you and to associate your profile and match history with your account.
  • Username is displayed to other players during matchmaking, in the lobby, and on the friend list.
  • Push token is used only to deliver in-app challenge notifications (e.g. "Your friend challenged you to a game").
  • Gameplay data is used to run multiplayer sessions in real time.
  • Social data is used to display your friend list and manage incoming/outgoing game challenges.

3. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — used for authentication, real-time game synchronisation, and database storage. Data is processed in accordance with Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — used to deliver push notifications. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini AI — used to generate pixel-art avatars from selfies. The selfie image is transmitted to Google solely for this purpose and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Data is shared with the third-party services listed above only to the extent necessary to operate the App.

Your username is visible to other players you interact with inside the App (friend requests, lobby, matchmaking). No other personal data is exposed to other players.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your account and associated data (profile, friend list, match history) are retained as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the address below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including TLS encryption in transit and hashed password storage, to protect your data. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
